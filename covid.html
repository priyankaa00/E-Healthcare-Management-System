PRECAUTIONS
to prevent the spread of COVID-19:



  1. 01

    Clean your hands often. Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub.

  2. 02

    Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth.

  3. 03

    Maintain at least a 1-metre distance between yourself and others to reduce your risk of infection when they cough, sneeze or speak.

  4. 04

    Make wearing a mask a normal part of being around other people. The appropriate use, storage and cleaning or disposal are essential to make masks as effective as possible.

  5. 05

    Stay home and self-isolate even if you have minor symptoms such as cough, headache, mild fever, until you recover.

Healthcare Tips
Coronavirus Quarantine

Physical Activity

By doing 3-4 minutes of light intensity physical movement, such as walking or stretching, will help ease your muscles and improve blood circulation and muscle activity.

Healthy Diet

Every day, eat a mix of wholegrains like wheat, maize and rice, legumes like lentils and beans, plenty of fresh fruit and vegetables , with some foods from animal sources.

Vaccination

COVID-19 vaccination will help protect you by creating an antibody (immune system) response without having to experience sickness.
